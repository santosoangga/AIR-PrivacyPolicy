# Privacy Policy

**Mobile Apps Privacy Policy**

*Last Updated: May 5, 2026*

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications. Our apps are designed for field employees to submit proof of work through photos and location data.

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect the following information through our apps:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system, unique device identifier
- **Photos:** Images you submit as proof of field work
- **Location Data:** GPS coordinates and location information when you submit job-related data
- **Usage Data:** App activity, timestamps, and submission history

## 3. Account Activation

Our apps do not require traditional user registration with usernames and passwords. Instead:

- Employees receive activation codes from their company administrator
- Employees enter the activation code to link their device to their employee account
- No personal contact information is collected during the activation process

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

- Verify and process field job submissions
- Track and record work locations and timestamps
- Maintain accurate work records for compliance purposes
- Provide admin access to submitted proofs for review
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## 5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

- All data is encrypted during transmission
- Submitted photos and location data are securely stored
- Access to employee data is restricted to authorized administrative personnel only
- We regularly review and update our security practices

## 6. Data Retention

Photos and location data are retained for business purposes and compliance requirements. Data retention periods follow company policies and applicable legal obligations.

## 7. Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data. To submit a data deletion request:

- Contact your company administrator directly
- Provide your employee identification details for verification
- Specify which data you want deleted
- Your request will be processed according to company procedures and applicable laws

## 8. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services for functionality such as:

- Cloud storage for data backups
- Analytics to improve app performance
- Maps and location services

These third-party providers have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their terms.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and, if applicable, through the company administrator.

## 11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or need assistance with data deletion requests, please contact your company administrator or reach out through official company channels.
